Q5 – 12‑bit Two’s‑Complement Representations

1. –13
   +13 in binary:    0000 0000 1101
   invert bits:      1111 1111 0010
   add 1:            1111 1111 0011
   → 111111110011

2. 56
   56 = 32+16+8 →    0000 0011 1000
   → 000000111000

3. –1
   +1:               0000 0000 0001
   invert bits:      1111 1111 1110
   add 1:            1111 1111 1111
   → 111111111111

4. –2048
   Most negative:    1000 0000 0000
   → 100000000000

5. 2048
   Out of range for 12 bits (can only represent –2048…+2047)
   → N/A
